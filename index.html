Yang Liu 刘洋

EN

Hi, I am Yang Liu. I am a Research Scientist/Engineer on Tencent's HY post-training team, focusing on context agents and long-context LLMs. I am also a Postdoctoral Researcher at Tsinghua University, working on post-training applications. I received my Ph.D. from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, specializing in graph and combinatorial optimization, large language models, and reasoning. I received my Bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University and have conducted research visits at the University of Alberta, Michigan State University, and Peking University.

中

My Reflection / 我的忍道

科研之路如同修行——看似孤独的探索背后，是对信念的坚守；

每一篇论文、每一次实验，都是一场不确定结局的任务；

但正如鸣人所说，真正的强大不在于天赋，而在于永不言弃的意志；

无论是面对学术中的迷雾，还是生活里的风雨，我都选择向前奔跑，因为——

这就是我的忍道。
