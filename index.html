Yang Liu 刘洋

EN

Hi, I am Yang Liu. I am a Member of Technical Staff at Tencent's HY Post-Training Team, focusing on (long-)context agents and LLMs. I am also a Postdoctoral Researcher at Tsinghua University, working on LLM applications and science.

I received my Ph.D. from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I was advised by Chuan Zhou. During my Ph.D., I spent four years working on graph and combinatorial optimization, large language models, and reasoning. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University and conducted research visits at the University of Alberta, Michigan State University, and Peking University.

Goal: LLMs that improve everyone's life (society & science).
