Yang Liu 刘洋

Hi, I am Yang Liu. I am a Research Scientist/Engineer on Tencent's HY post-training team, focusing on context/social agents and long-context LLMs. I am also a Postdoctoral Researcher at Tsinghua University, working on post-training applications. I received my Ph.D. from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, specializing in graph and combinatorial optimization, large language models, and reasoning. I received my Bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University and have conducted research visits at the University of Alberta, Michigan State University, and Peking University. I have also interned at Seed@Bytedance, Hunyuan@Tencent, Code@Zhipu, and CCL@Baidu.
