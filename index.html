Hi, I am Yang Liu (刘洋). I am a Ph.D. student at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, specializing in Graph Mining and Optimization, with a focus on Large Language Models (LLMs). Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University and have conducted research visits at the University of Alberta, Michigan State University, and Peking University.

Now, I am a Qingyun intern at Hunyuan from Tencent, working on LLM post-train at Beijing, China!

Looking for research collaborators on meaningful projects and feel free to contact with me if you are interested! Also, I'm in job market and actively seeking industry positions.
