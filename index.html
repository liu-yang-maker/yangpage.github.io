Yang Liu 刘洋

Hi, I am Yang Liu. I am an incoming Research Scientist/Engineer at a LLM Foundation Team, working on Post-training and Long Context LLMs. Also, I will join Tsinghua University as a Postdoctoral Researcher, working on Post-training Applications. Now, I am a final-year Ph.D. student at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, specializing in Graph Mining and Optimization, with a focus on Large Language Models (LLMs). Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University and have conducted research visits at the University of Alberta, Michigan State University, and Peking University.
